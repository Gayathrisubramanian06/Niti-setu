# Niti-Setu: 1-Minute Pitch Speech

**Estimated time: ~1 Minute (approx. 145 words)**

---

"Hello everyone, I'm excited to present **Niti-Setu**. 

Did you know millions of farmers miss out on life-changing government schemes simply because the policies are too complex, scattered, and hard to understand? Niti-Setu is built to solve exactly this.

**What is it?** Niti-Setu is an intelligent policy-bridge platform designed to connect farmers directly with the government schemes they deserve. 

**How does it help?** A farmer can simply input their profile details—even using a simple voice command—and our system instantly analyzes complex policy documents. Instead of giving them more documents to read, we give them a clear **'YES'** or **'NO'** eligibility result. We also provide the exact reason and a proof citation, all presented in a simple, farmer-friendly dashboard.

**Under the hood**, our platform is powered by a modern tech stack. We use **React** for our intuitive frontend, **Node.js and Express** for our backend, and **MongoDB** for our database. The core brain of Niti-Setu is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (or **RAG**) pipeline, powered by the **Gemini API**, which accurately matches farmer profiles against real government scheme documents.

Thank you!"

---

### Key Points Hit:
- **What is it?** A bridge platform for farmers and government schemes.
- **Why is it needed?** To simplify complex policies that cause farmers to miss out on benefits.
- **How is it helpful?** Allows easy input (even voice) and provides direct YES/NO eligibility with transparent reasons/citations without overwhelming the user.
- **Tech Stack:** React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Gemini API (Generative AI / RAG engine).
